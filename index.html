The Crystal Collector

How to Play

You will be given a target number at the start of each game.

Your goal is to click on the crystals below until your score equals the given number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the given number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the given number.

Each time the game starts, the crystals will get a new value.
